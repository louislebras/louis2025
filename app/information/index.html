Louis Le Bras is a designer, front-end engineer and psychology researcher in neuroscience and monetary systems.

He founded and led Donzelli®, a curated platform where furniture, places and architecture people meet. He also created Principle OS™, a radical operating system to craft everything on the web in few days with AI with high customization including design systems engineering and more — to bring to life and materialize the ideas he envisions without limits, with his will as the only obstacle.

He has been working with brands, agencies and startups for almost 9 years cumulated. Selected — Nike, Kitchen Aid, FFF, Fraser Yachts... and had the pleasure to challenge them in their conversion system, client journey and brand experience.

About
Alongside design, Louis works on issues of sovereignty, investment, and security through Bitcoin. His long-term vision is to open a dedicated Bitcoin Family Office for private investors, helping them become their own bank. The mission: build tailored strategies around their Bitcoin wealth (not other cryptocurrencies), covering acquisition, sale, self-custody, hardware, inheritance, transmission, growth, and placement. In order to provide a true experience of guidance on Bitcoin: Investment, Security & Knowledge base.
